Who am I?
Good question! I have no idea anymore. This used to be a big paragraph talking about university and my interest in computer science but recently I've not really been quite sure about what I want in life. I can't really put it into words but I'm trying to make myself happy. I really want it to work out in the end.

On a more literal and less apocalyptic note, I like to spend my time making, consuming or thinking about art. Drawing and music have always been big parts of my life, so I'll usually be working on music digitally or by practising guitar, and drawing in notebooks and Clip Studio, all of which I'd love for you to see and enjoy on this site.

I also enjoy gardening - which IS an art! - watching and discussing movies, playing videogames, cooking, building Gundam, collecting books, albums and magazines
